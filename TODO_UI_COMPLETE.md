## Design Philosophy

The design follows Apple's Human Interface Guidelines:
- **Clarity** - Clear hierarchy, readable text
- **Deference** - UI doesn't compete with content
- **Depth** - Subtle shadows and layering
- **Simplicity** - Minimal elements, maximum clarity
